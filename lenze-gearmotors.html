LENZE Gearmotors FAQ

Q: Are your drive solutions with original LENZE® products?
A: Yes. Our industrial gearmotors and drive components are brand new and original. The mounting dimensions, shaft diameters, and technical ratings perfectly according to the spare parts.

Q: What are the main advantages of Lenze g500 series gearboxes?
A: The Lenze g500 series features high energy efficiency (up to 94% or higher), low backlash, and compact design. It includes Inline Helical (g500-H), Parallel Shaft (g500-S), Helical Bevel (g500-B), and Helical Worm (g500-W) setups to meet diverse torque and space requirements.

Q: Can you provide matching frequency inverters and smart motors for automated lines?
A: Absolute. Besides standard AC motors (m550-P/IE3), we supply Lenze-compatible Smart Motors (m300 series) optimized for conveyor systems, as well as high-precision frequency inverters (i500/i550 series) for precise variable speed control.

Q: Which industrial sectors are these Lenze-type geared motors ideal for?
A: They are widely engineered for high-frequency stop-and-go industries, including material handling, intralogistics, conveyor networks, automotive assembly lines, packaging machinery, and automated food processing.
