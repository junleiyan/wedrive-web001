ODM Gearmotors FAQ

Q: What custom ODM services do you offer for gearmotors?
A: We offer comprehensive ODM services including custom torque ratings, specific gear ratios, private labeling (your brand), and housing design adjustments to meet your unique industrial requirements.

Q: Can you replace famous brands with your ODM solutions?
A: Yes. Our ODM gearmotors are designed to be dimensionally interchangeable with major European and international brands, providing a cost-effective, high-quality alternative under your own brand or ours.

Q: What is the typical lead time for an ODM project?
A: For standard ODM modifications, lead times are significantly shorter than major brands. Custom technical designs usually take 4-8 weeks depending on the complexity of the power transmission specifications.

Q: How do you ensure the quality of ODM power transmission products?
A: Every ODM unit undergoes rigorous load testing, noise level checks, and precision alignment inspections. Wedrive Power ensures all custom products meet international industrial durability standards.
