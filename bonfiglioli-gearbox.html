BONFIGLIOLI Gearbox FAQ

Q: Are your gear units and 300M series planetary gearboxes original BONFIGLIOLI® products?
A: Yes. All our heavy-duty power transmission units and 300M series planetary gearboxes are brand new and 100% original. Mounting configurations, shaft specifications, and torque ratings align perfectly with genuine Bonfiglioli engineering standards.

Q: What are the main features of the Bonfiglioli 300M Series Planetary Gearbox?
A: The Bonfiglioli 300M series is optimized for high-torque industrial applications, offering exceptional power density, modular gear configurations, and maximum shock-load resistance for severe-duty industrial operations.

Q: What standard output configurations do you supply for Bonfiglioli gearmotors?
A: We offer comprehensive standard design variants, including Solid Shaft (with parallel keys or splines), Hollow Shaft with shrink disks, and Splined Hollow Shafts compatible with Bonfiglioli A, F, C, and 300M series.

Q: Which heavy industries benefit most from Bonfiglioli 300M series planetary drives?
A: These high-efficiency industrial planetary drives are heavily utilized in mining equipment, marine winches, sugar mills, cement plant crushers, large material conveyors, and waste treatment facilities.
