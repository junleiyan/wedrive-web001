Structure of Gear Unit Designation (Example: B3SH11A16)

  • B: Bevel-helical gear units
  • 3: No. of stages (3 stages)
  • S: Solid shaft with parallel key
  • H: Horizontal mounting position
  • 11: Gear unit size
  • A: Version A
  • 16: Ratio i = 16
