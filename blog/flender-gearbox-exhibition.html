FLENDER Gearbox Detail

CMS-DX PRO

Our specialized gearbox solutions are engineered to meet the rigorous demands of modern lifting operations, featuring a high torque and lightweight design that optimizes power density for superior performance. These units provide exceptional high shock load resistance, specifically built to withstand extreme operational stress in demanding environments. Additionally, the extended center distance allows for flexible layout configurations and enhanced load distribution. These gearboxes are ideal for a wide range of critical applications, including various hoisting mechanisms as well as luffing or boom systems, ensuring reliability and precision in heavy-duty lifting tasks. .

1. Flender Unveils the All-New High-Efficiency FLENDER ONE Gearbox with Universal 6-Sided Mounting

    The new FLENDER ONE gearbox features universal 6-sided mounting support for all lubrication configurations, offering unparalleled installation flexibility. Engineered for superior performance, it delivers significantly higher operational efficiency and extended bearing service life. The system is designed with intelligence at its core, ensuring the gearbox is both easy to install and simple to maintain. These advanced units are versatile enough to serve over 100 different applications, making them an ideal choice for material handling equipment, cranes, mixing systems, and many more industrial processes.

2. FLENDER SIP: Standard Industrial Planetary Gearboxes

The SIP standard industrial planetary gearbox features a modular design that ensures a compact structure, providing engineers with maximum integration flexibility. Engineered for high performance, it delivers increased gear tooth contact area for smoother, more stable operation during heavy-duty tasks. Furthermore, the unit is built to be highly energy-efficient, contributing to sustainable and cost-effective industrial processes. These gearboxes are ideal for a wide range of demanding applications, including travel drives, car dumper traction and positioning drives, and grate cooler drives, ensuring reliability and precision across all operations.
