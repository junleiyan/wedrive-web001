Modular Products + NORD Expertise: From Systems to Solutions

In industrial production, drive systems are the "heart" of the operation. As a long-standing supplier in the drive industry, NORD leverages its modular product portfolio and extensive industry experience to provide customized solutions for a wide range of sectors. As a developer of drive solutions, NORD offers all components of drive technology through its modular product series, which can be combined into various configurations. With profound technical expertise, we provide tailor-made drive solutions for applications across diverse industries.

Increasing Energy Efficiency with NORD

NORD Expertise: Providing Customized Drive Solutions

NORD continues to set industry standards by offering all components of drive technology through its modular product series. These components can be combined into various configurations to meet the specific requirements of our clients. With profound technical expertise, we provide tailor-made drive solutions for applications across diverse industries, ensuring that our customers benefit from both high-performance hardware and intelligent software integration.

1. Setting Global Standards for Energy Efficiency

  • Global Compatibility: NORD motors are designed for global use and comply with all relevant international energy efficiency regulations.
  • IE5+ Technology: Our IE5+ synchronous motors not only meet the current IE5 efficiency requirements but also deliver superior energy performance in practical industrial applications.

2. Driving Industrial Automation through IIoT and Smart Maintenance

Through Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) technology, users can utilize the PLC integrated into NORD frequency inverters to acquire and analyze drive and status data. This capability enables the collection of critical information required for predictive maintenance, thereby significantly increasing the overall efficiency and reliability of the entire industrial system. NORD’s commitment to intelligence ensures that our drive systems are not just hardware components, but active contributors to modern smart factory operations.

3. Professional Support and Service

NORD provides comprehensive service offerings, including the NORD ECO efficiency analysis, to help customers optimize their energy usage. Our technical experts are dedicated to assisting clients in selecting the best drive configurations to meet their specific operational goals.

4. Looking Ahead: NORD’s Commitment to Innovation
