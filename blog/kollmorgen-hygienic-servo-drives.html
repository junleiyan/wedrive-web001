Kollmorgen Highly Efficient Servo Drives Bring High Performance to Washdown and Hygienic Environments

Kollmorgen, a global leader in motion control and automation solutions, is expanding the application of its Kollmorgen Highly Efficient Servo Drives (KED) to hygienic and washdown environments. The KED is designed for simplicity, flexibility, quick commissioning, and reliable performance. By pairing these high-efficiency drives with AKMA stainless steel washdown motors and AKMH hygienic stainless steel servo motors, machine builders can now achieve high-performance motion control in demanding washdown applications without the excessive costs typically associated with such systems.

The Kollmorgen Highly Efficient Servo Drives feature out-of-the-box support for multiple industrial Ethernet protocols, including EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, and PROFINET, providing extensive connectivity while reducing system and inventory complexity.

Efficient Absolute Feedback for Washdown Applications

To further enhance value, AKMA and AKMH motors can now be equipped with SFD-M (Smart Feedback Device, Multiturn), enabling Kollmorgen Highly Efficient Servo Drives to support absolute multiturn positioning in hygienic and washdown environments. SFD-M provides high-resolution absolute feedback without the costs and maintenance associated with battery-backed encoders. Its energy-harvesting technology stores position information through power cycles, while embedded motor data enables automatic drive configuration, speeding up commissioning for all Kollmorgen servo drives, including KED, AKD, and AKD2G.

"High-resolution, absolute multiturn feedback has traditionally required an upgrade to more expensive servo systems," says Chris Cooper, Director of Global Product Management. "By integrating SFD-M into our AKMA and AKMH motors and pairing them with Kollmorgen's high-performance servo drives, we offer the same precision and control in an efficient solution specifically designed for food and beverage, pharmaceutical packaging, and other washdown applications."

Designed for Washdown and Hygienic Environments

AKMA motors feature an anodized aluminum housing with an IP69K protection rating, suitable for moderate to heavy washdown applications. AKMH motors feature an IP69K sealed stainless steel construction, ideal for daily washdown environments involving high-pressure, high-temperature, and corrosive cleaning agents. By utilizing SFD-M in these expanded motor families, Kollmorgen provides a unified 24-bit absolute feedback solution that delivers consistent precision across diverse environments. Combined with Kollmorgen Highly Efficient Servo Drives, machine builders gain high-performance motion control solutions they can trust in washdown and hygienic applications.
