ABB’s improved Baldor-Reliance® Food Safe stainless steel motors bring a higher level of reliability and cleanability

Published on: February 20, 2023 | Location: Fort Smith, Arkansas

ABB Bringing you the latest brand news and industry information. in Global.

“

Food safety is the first priority of design.

”

Reliability in intense cleaning environments

Now, more than ever, it is vital that food and beverage companies choose supplier partners that understand their processes, helping to improve plant reliability, sustainability and achieve productivity goals. For more than three decades, ABB has listened to equipment designers, operators and plant maintenance engineers to design and manufacture Baldor-Reliance Food Safe motors to perform reliably, at maximum efficiency, around the clock - and able to withstand rigorous cleaning and sanitation processes. Now ABB has underscored its commitment to uncompromised food safety and productivity with key enhancements in performance and protection to its Food Safe stainless steel motor line.

Enhanced design and Features that have stood the test of time

Enhanced design features include a bearing protection ring to reduce shaft currents, a 360-degree rotatable conduit box for ease of installation, operation and maintenance, and plugged drain holes to completely seal the motor and minimize water ingress. Features that have stood the test of time include all stainless-steel construction—housing, conduit box, drain plugs, hardware, fan cover and endplates—safeguarding against corrosion to avoid costly downtime and repairs. Additionally, an IP69 laser marked nameplate ensures readability, hardened epoxy resin encapsulation provides internal protection against water and humidity, and two-barrier mechanical shaft seals prevent water from entering at the shaft extension. High-temperature Class H insulation with low-temperature (Class B) rise ensures longer insulation and bearing life.

Motor Versatility

Baldor-Reliance Food Safe motors, designed with smooth contours and advanced sealing, exceed IP69 ingress protection ratings for washdown conditions to maximize motor life.

  • Configuration: Available in foot-mounted and footless configurations.
  • Flexibility: Covers single to three-phase ratings, with TEFC or TENV enclosures.
  • Sustainability: High reliability reduces downtime, waste, and CO2, protecting the environment.
  • Industry Leadership: ABB is a technology leader in electrification and automation.

Commitment to Sustainability

“We work closely with our customers to understand their needs and to stay informed on the emerging trends in the food industry. The top priorities in food production equipment right now are cleanability and durability,” said Matt Rodebush, Global Segment Manager, Food & Beverage, for ABB’s NEMA Motors Division. “If a motor is easier to clean and maintain, we increase the safety of our food supply. It’s that simple. But our customers don’t just want equipment that’s easy to clean; they need motors that can stand up to those harsh cleaning conditions and keep going. We’ve addressed both requests with the enhanced design of our Food Safe stainless steel motors.”

1. Achieving Sustainability Targets

When selecting the highest quality motor suitable for harsh washdown applications, it’s possible to achieve sustainability targets of food processing plants. High reliability means high productivity while reducing downtime, waste and CO2 - creating a safe, sustainable solution that protects the environment.
