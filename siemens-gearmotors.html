SIEMENS 1LE0 Series - Order No. & Motor Type Decoding Guide

Technical guide for parsing the 16-digit Order No. of Siemens low-voltage motor series.

Category Digit Pos. Code & Meaning
Series & Housing 1 - 4 1LE0: Low-voltage motor series
Digit 4: 0 = Cast Iron, 3 = Aluminium
Efficiency Class 5 3 = Premium Efficiency (China Energy Efficiency Grade 3)
Frame Size 6 - 7 0D=80; 0E=90; 1A=100; 1B=112; 1C=132; 1D=160; 1E=180;
2A=200; 2B=225; 2C=250; 2D=280; 3A=315; 3B=355
Poles 8 A=2; B=4; C=6; D=8
Voltage & Frequency 11 - 12 04 = 400 VD 50 Hz; 22 = 230 VD/400 VY 50 Hz;
34 = 400 VD/690 VY 50 Hz; 90 = Special voltage & frequency
Mounting Type 14 A=IM B3; J=IM B35; F=IM B5; K=IM B14; G=IM V1; V=IM B8; H=IM V3
Winding Protection 15 A=No protection; B=3xPTC (tripping); C=6xPTC (alarm+tripping);
H=3xPt100; J=6xPt100 temperature sensors
