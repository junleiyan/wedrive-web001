Sumitomo & Hansen Gearbox FAQ

Q: Are your heavy-duty drive solutions with original Sumitomo and Hansen products?
A: Yes. All our industrial gearboxes, speed reducers, and power transmission components are brand new and 100% original. The output shaft dimensions, torque ratings, and center distances align perfectly with the factory technical specifications.

Q: What makes Sumitomo Cyclo® drives ideal for heavy shock loads?
A: Unlike standard helical gears, the Sumitomo Cyclo® 6000 series utilizes cycloidal pinwheel technology, sharing the load across 2/3 of the reduction components simultaneously. This unique design provides exceptional reliability and up to 500% shock overload capacity without catastrophic failure.

Q: Can you supply standard replacement parts for Hansen P4 and Paramax® 9000 series?
A: Absolutely. We supply high-torque Sumitomo Paramax® 9000 industrial gearboxes and compatible Hansen P4 multi-stage units. These heavy-duty gear units are engineered with superior thermal ratings and modular shaft orientations for easy field replacement.

Q: Which industrial sectors rely heavily on Sumitomo and Hansen industrial drives?
A: These heavy-duty gearboxes are widely engineered for demanding, continuous-duty applications including mining conveyors, cement kilns, port lifting machinery, cooling tower fans, agitators, water treatment aerators, and pulp and paper processing plants.
