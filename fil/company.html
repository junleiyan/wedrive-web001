Kadalubhasaan sa Sektor

Ang aming kadalubhasaan sa engineering ay sumasaklaw sa malawak na pang-industriyang landscape, na tinitiyak ang katumpakan at pagiging maaasahan sa mga mahihirap na kapaligiran:

  • Enerhiya at Imprastraktura: Wind power, nuclear energy, water conservancy, at mga proyektong pang-bagong enerhiya.
  • Mabigat na Industriya: Malalaking industrial gearboxes, marine engineering, mining machinery, at heavy port logistics.
  • Automation at Motion Control: Servo motors, high-torque steering systems, actuators, at precision couplings.
  • Logistics at Manufacturing: Automotive production lines, high-load conveyor systems, at mga espesyal na worm gear.

Pinagsamang Global Supply Chain
