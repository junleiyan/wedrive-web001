NEUGART Gearbox FAQ

Q: Are your gear units and planetary gearboxes original NEUGART® products?
A: Yes. All our heavy-duty power transmission units and planetary gearboxes are brand new and 100% original. Mounting configurations, shaft specifications, and torque ratings align perfectly with genuine Neugart engineering standards.

Q: What are the main features of the Neugart Planetary Series?
A: The Neugart planetary series is optimized for high-precision and high-torque industrial applications, offering exceptional power density, modular gear configurations, and maximum shock-load resistance for severe-duty operations.

Q: What standard output configurations do you supply for Neugart planetary gearboxes?
A: We offer comprehensive standard design variants, including Solid Shaft (with parallel keys or splines), Hollow Shaft with shrink disks, and Splined Hollow Shafts compatible with Neugart planetary series.

Q: Which heavy industries benefit most from Neugart planetary drives?
A: These high-efficiency industrial planetary drives are heavily utilized in automation equipment, robotics, packaging machinery, machine tools, and large material handling systems.
