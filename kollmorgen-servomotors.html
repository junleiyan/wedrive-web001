Kollmorgen Servomotors FAQ

Q: Are your motion solutions with original Kollmorgen® products?
A: Yes. All our Kollmorgen brushless servo motors and direct-drive systems are 100% brand new and original. The mounting flange sizes, shaft configurations, and technical specifications precisely match the factory replacement parts.

Q: What are the main technical advantages of Kollmorgen AKM servo motors?
A: The Kollmorgen AKM® series delivers industry-leading power density, high dynamic responsiveness, and low inertia. They offer an exceptionally wide range of standard models (AKM1 to AKM8) with flexible feedback options (SFD, BiSS, EnDat, Resolver) to meet diverse high-precision torque and speed automation needs.

Q: Do you provide complete motion control setups including servo drives?
A: Absolutely. In addition to high-performance brushless motors, we supply matched Kollmorgen-compatible servo drives (such as the AKD® / AKD2G series). These are optimized for multi-axis synchronization, frameless motor setups, and direct-drive rotary systems.

Q: Which industrial automation sectors are Kollmorgen servomotors ideal for?
A: They are widely engineered for high-precision, demanding industrial fields including robotics, AGVs, semiconductor manufacturing, aerospace testing, medical devices, high-speed packaging machinery, and automated food processing lines requiring washdown capabilities.
