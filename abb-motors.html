ABB Industrial Motors FAQ

1. What industries use ABB industrial motors?

ABB industrial motors are widely used in cement, mining, oil & gas, power generation, water treatment, ports, lifting systems, chemicals, food processing and wind power industries.

2. Do you supply original ABB motors?

Yes. WEDRIVE POWER supplies 100% original and brand-new ABB industrial motors, including low voltage motors, high voltage motors and explosion proof motors.

3. Can you help select the correct ABB motor model?

Yes. Please provide the motor nameplate, torque, power, speed, voltage, application details or project requirements. Our engineers can assist with model selection and replacement solutions.

4. What is the delivery time for ABB motors?

Delivery time depends on ABB factory production schedules and stock availability. Some standard models are available quickly, while customized motors may require additional lead time.

5. Do you provide ABB explosion proof motors?

Yes. We supply ABB explosion proof motors for hazardous areas, including IEC and NEMA standard motors for industrial applications.

6. Can ABB motors replace old Siemens, SEW or Baldor motors?

In many applications, ABB motors can be used as replacement solutions. Please provide technical parameters and installation dimensions for verification.
