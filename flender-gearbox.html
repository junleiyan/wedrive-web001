FLENDER Gear Unit Nomenclature & Coding Standards

Understanding the exact nomenclature parameters ensures perfect fitments during part number replication or plant replacement procurement cycles.

Structure of Gear Unit Designation (Example: B3SH11A16)

  • B: Bevel-helical gear units
  • 3: No. of stages (3 stages)
  • S: Solid shaft with parallel key
  • H: Horizontal mounting position
  • 11: Gear unit size identifier
  • A: Structural Version A
  • 16: Nominal Ratio i = 16

FLENDER Gearbox FAQ

Q: Are your industrial gear units fully original FLENDER® products?
A: Yes. Our heavy-duty power transmission gearboxes are the ariginal and brand new.Mounting dimensions, shaft configurations, and technical specifications same as FLENDER H and B series.

Q: What is the difference between FLENDER Helical H series and Bevel-Helical B series?
A: Helical H series features parallel shafts for standard inline configurations, while Bevel-Helical B series utilizes right-angle intersecting shafts, ideal for 90° drive designs.

Q: What options are available for the output shaft configurations?
A: We support comprehensive standard designs including Solid Shaft (S), Hollow Shaft with parallel key (H), Hollow Shaft with shrink disk (D), and Hollow Shaft with splines.

Q: Which heavy industries are these high-torque gear units engineered for?
A: They are highly optimized for severe operational environments including cement processing, mining crushers, heavy port lifting, conveyors, and water treatment machinery.
